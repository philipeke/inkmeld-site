Last updated: April 2026. This page explains how cookies and related technologies may be used to improve the InkMeld website experience.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device that help websites remember preferences, understand usage patterns, and improve performance.

How We Use Cookies

  • To support core website behavior and remember simple preferences.
  • To understand how visitors use the site and where improvements are needed.
  • To help maintain the quality and reliability of support and product pages.

Analytics

InkMeld may use services such as Firebase Analytics or related analytics tooling to understand how the site is used. These tools can involve cookies or similar technologies.
