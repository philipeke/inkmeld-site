Fast Answers

The questions that show up most.

How do credits work?

InkMeld supports a monthly quota plus purchased AI credits for heavier processing. Purchase validation is designed to happen server-side before grants are applied.

The cutout missed pieces of the design. What should I do?

Try a source image with clearer contrast and less compression. Clean artwork with a strong silhouette usually produces the best isolation results.

My tattoo looks like it is floating over the skin.

Reduce opacity slightly, then increase skin blend, edge softness, and a touch of shadow. Those controls are there specifically to push the design into the body instead of keeping it flat.

Can I use a picture I found online?

Yes. The app is built around importing from many sources. Just make sure you have the rights you need before turning reference material into a real tattoo.
