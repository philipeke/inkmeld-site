Frequently Asked Questions


How do credits work?

Your account receives a monthly quota of credits. If you run out, you can purchase more directly via the app store.


The AI cutout missed a spot, what do I do?

Make sure the photo of the stencil or design has good contrast. You can also re-run the cutout with the "High Precision" toggle enabled.
