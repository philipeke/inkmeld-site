Account Management

Delete your InkMeld account

If you want to permanently remove your InkMeld account and associated data, use the in-app flow or send a deletion request from the email address linked to your account.

Important

Account deletion is intended to be permanent. If you simply need help accessing your account, contact support first.

Delete from the app

  1. Open the InkMeld app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Open Account Management.
  4. Select Delete Account and confirm the request.

Delete by email

If you cannot access the app, send your deletion request from the email address associated with the account. We recommend including the subject line "Delete InkMeld account".

Need help instead?

If the issue is billing, login, or credits rather than permanent deletion, use the Support Hub and we will help you recover the account first.
