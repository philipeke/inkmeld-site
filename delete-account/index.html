We're sad to see you go

If you'd like to completely remove your account and all associated data from InkMeld AI, you can do so directly from the mobile app or request it here.


How to delete via App

  1. Open the InkMeld app and go to Settings.
  2. Tap on "Account Management".
  3. Select "Delete Account".
  4. Confirm your choice. Your data will be deleted within 30 days.

Request Deletion

If you no longer have access to the app, you can enter your email to request deletion.
