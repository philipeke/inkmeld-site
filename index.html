FAQ

Questions people ask before they let the future near their skin.

The goal is not to replace tattoo artists. The goal is to arrive at the artist with far better signal.

Can I really use an image from the internet as a tattoo starting point?

Yes. InkMeld is built for reference chaos. Import a web image, isolate the object you care about with AI, then move into the preview editor to see how that exact visual behaves on your own body photo.

What can I adjust once the tattoo is placed?

Placement, scale, rotation, tint, brightness, contrast, saturation, hue, opacity, edge softness, micro blur, grain, shadow, highlight, and skin blend are all part of the editing philosophy.

Is the preview only for black ink?

No. InkMeld includes tint handling and multiple material presets. That means you can push the preview toward dense blackwork, softer ink behavior, futuristic neon energy, or hard reflective finishes.

How do credits work?

The app supports a monthly quota plus purchased AI credits. Commerce flows are designed with server-side receipt verification so credit grants happen only after validation.

Can I save or share my result with an artist?

Yes. The product direction already includes save, export, and share behavior so your best previews can move from experiment to real conversation.
